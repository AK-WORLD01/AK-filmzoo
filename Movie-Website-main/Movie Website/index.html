Bade Miyan Chote Miyan (2024) is a high-octane Bollywood action thriller directed by Ali Abbas Zafar, featuring powerhouse performers Akshay Kumar and Tiger Shroff in the lead roles. The film follows two elite Indian Army officers on a global mission to stop a mysterious villain who has stolen “Karan Kavach”—a dangerous AI-powered defense weapon that threatens national security. Packed with thrilling stunts, explosive action, and international locations, the movie blends cutting-edge technology themes with patriotic spirit. Also starring Prithviraj Sukumaran, Sonakshi Sinha, Manushi Chhillar, and Alaya F, Bade Miyan Chote Miyan delivers large-scale entertainment for fans of high-energy action cinema.

"Fateh" is a gripping action-thriller directed by and starring Sonu Sood, set against the backdrop of modern-day cybercrime in India. The film follows Fateh Singh, a former special ops officer, who is drawn back into action when a small-town girl becomes a victim of a ruthless digital scam. With the help of an ethical hacker, he embarks on a high-stakes mission to expose the syndicate behind the operation.
